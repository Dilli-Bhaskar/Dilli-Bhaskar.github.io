Design Analysis of Detroit 8V-71 Marine Diesel Engine Piston

• Re-designed Detroit 8V-71 Engine using Convex and Concave Profiles for Piston head.
• Using Ansys Workbench static stress analysis was performed and observed that Concave Head profile shows better result than Flat Head profile and Dome Head profile in terms of stresses and total deformations.
