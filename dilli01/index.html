About Me

I’m a Prefinal Year undergrad in Mechanical Engineering at TCE. Currently I am working at Artificial Intelligence lab supervised by Dr. M.Suguna. Here, I built a presice object detectors on Themal images, and currently I am working on reducing the computational cost of turbulent fluid flow simulation around a long cylindrical structures using deep learning.

I am also interested in the learning dynamics of Deep Learning agents, using mathematical tools from optimization, dynamical systems and learning theory to understand what RL agents learn (and fail to learn).
